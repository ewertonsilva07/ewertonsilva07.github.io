Ping Pong Score Keeper

Description:

This app will count the score of both ping pong players until one of them reaches the selected final score.

Functionalities:

  • Select how many points you want the game to end.
  • Use the player buttons to add points until one of the players reach the final score selected
  • The players buttons will disable when one player reaches the final score
  • Use the Reset button to start a new game
  • The score display will show which player lost and who won
  • The display will reset if the final score is changed in the middle of a game
